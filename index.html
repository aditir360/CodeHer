CodeHer

Empowering Women to Code with Confidence and Purpose

Welcome

Welcome to our platform, CodeHer. It is designed to help empower women to take part in computer science. Only 20% of women work in the STEM workforce. It's neccessary to bring this percentage up, bringing equality and justice to all.

How does CodeHer work?

CodeHer fufills it's goal of fostering a passion for computer science in women through it's 4 main features:



  • CodeCoach: An multilingual AI Assistant that offers personalized advice and support. It also provides guidance for learning coding skills. Helps increase awarneness of the equality issue in the computer science field among it's users.


  • CodeLibrary: A comprehensive resource hub offering a curated collection of websites, tutorials, and development tools to facilitate learning and efficient coding practices.


  • CodeStars: A showcase of exceptional women in technology, highlighting their achievements, stories, and contributions to the tech industry to inspire and empower others.


Vision for the future

CodeHer envisions a future where women are equally represented in the tech industry, empowered to pursue their passions and create innovative solutions that positively impact the world. By providing a supportive community, resources, and opportunities, CodeHer aims to inspire and equip women to become leaders and changemakers in the digital landscape.
